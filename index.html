Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much does it cost to create a website?

    Each website creation project is unique, so it's difficult to determine the exact cost. However, there are some criteria that can be used for an approximate estimate. Type of Website: A website with more pages and functionality will be more complex and therefore more expensive than a simpler site. For example, a corporate website will be cheaper than a site with a product catalog, and an e-commerce site with online shopping capabilities will be more complex and expensive. Development Approach: A website can be developed based on a ready-made template or with a custom design. In the first case, the cost and development time are significantly lower than in the second case. We provide free design templates for websites, which significantly simplify and speed up the website creation process. In most cases, template development makes the website creation process faster and significantly more cost-effective.

  • How long does it take to develop a website?

    A pre-designed WordPress site will typically take up to a month to complete. A custom-designed WordPress site will generally take up to 2 months. A custom-designed site on a framework will typically take around 3 months. However, these estimates are approximate. The complexity of the site can vary significantly, which can greatly affect the development timeline. For example, a simple corporate website with a pre-made design on WordPress can be developed in just a few weeks. On the other hand, an e-commerce site with a custom design on a framework can take several months.

  • What is a free audit?

    Liana Web Studio specialists will study all aspects of your website and promotion channels. After the audit is completed, the expert will provide you with a report detailing all the issues and opportunities for improvement. The report will also include recommendations for addressing issues or leveraging opportunities. Such an audit can be a great way to get an objective opinion about your website. It's also an opportunity to learn how to improve your site to attract more traffic and increase conversion. Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of a free website audit: Be prepared to provide information about your website. The expert conducting the audit will need information about your site, such as your marketing goals, target audience, and budget for promotion. Be open to feedback. It's important to be open to the specialist's opinion.

  • What does the website development process entail?

    The website development process includes several stages: Discussion and gathering requirements. Creating a prototype and approving the design. Development and programming. Testing and bug fixing. Launching the website and maintenance.

  • What content do I need to provide?

    To create a website, you will need to provide textual content, images, logos, and any other information you want to include on the site. We can assist you with creating and editing content if needed.

  • Will my website be responsive (mobile version)?

    Yes, all the websites we create are responsive and optimized for correct display on mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers.

  • Will it be possible to make changes to the website after its launch?

    Yes, we provide a content management system (CMS) that allows you to make changes to the website yourself after its launch. We also offer website support and update services.

  • Will my website be optimized for search engines (SEO)?

    Yes, we provide basic SEO optimization for your website to make it easily accessible and indexed by search engines. If you need more in-depth SEO optimization, we can offer additional services.

  • How will the payment be processed?

    We usually work in stages, with payment upon completion of each stage of the project. Payment can be divided into several parts: an advance payment, interim payments, and a final payment upon project completion.

  • What are the quality guarantees?

    We provide guarantees for all our work. If any issues related to our work arise within the agreed-upon period after the website launch, we will fix them free of charge.

  • Can I see examples of your work?

    Of course! You can view our previous projects in the portfolio section on our website. We can also provide additional examples and case studies upon request.

  • Will you provide technical support after the website launch?

    Yes, we offer services for technical support and maintenance of the website after its launch. This includes updates, bug fixes, adding new functionality, and other services upon your request.
